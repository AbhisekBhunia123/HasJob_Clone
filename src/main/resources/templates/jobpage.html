Job Description:

We are seeking a passionate Full Stack Developer with expertise in React.js, Next.js, and Node.js to join our dynamic team at Cognochain In this role, you will play a crucial part in building and maintaining our web platforms, crafting amazing UIs, and ensuring top-notch performance, scalability, and integration with our core AI technologies.

Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and maintain high-quality web applications using React.js, Next.js, and Node.js. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into functional solutions. Create beautiful, user-friendly UIs that enhance user experience and functionality. Integrate AI technologies and third-party APIs to expand the platform's capabilities. Optimize applications for performance, scalability, and security. Write clean, maintainable, and reusable code, adhering to best practices and coding standards. Debug and troubleshoot issues, providing timely resolution and improvements. Participate in code reviews and contribute to the continuous improvement of development processes.

Qualifications:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field. Proven experience developing web applications using React.js, Next.js, and Node.js. Strong understanding of web technologies, such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Experience with database design and management using MongoDB or other NoSQL databases. Familiarity with version control systems, such as Git. Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work both independently and collaboratively. Passion for coding and creating user-friendly, visually appealing UIs. Excellent communication and interpersonal skills. Join Cognochain and be part of a passionate team, shaping the future of mind-machine interface technology, and transforming industries through AI-driven innovations while creating impressive and user-friendly UIs.
